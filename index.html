Troubleshooting it all myself, problem-solving everything i stumbled upon, growing more and more curious about it. Finally, in early 2022 decided to move into proper software development. Main technologies learned there were: Javascript, HTML5, CSS3, NodeJS, ExpressJS, MongoDB, Mongoose, and many more. But more importantly, skills like problem-solving, adapting, and agile development. Moving into the tech world hence it has been my passion since i was a little child, started off with hardware, building my own pc and now slowly crawling my way into the software world.
